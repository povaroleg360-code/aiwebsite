About the Band

We Are Scientists is an indie rock band originally formed in California. The band became popular in the mid-2000s with energetic guitar music, humorous lyrics, and strong ties to the UK indie scene.

Their breakthrough album was With Love and Squalor which helped establish them as a major indie rock act.

Discography

Below are some of the most notable albums released by the band.

With Love and Squalor (2005)

The band's breakthrough album featuring songs like “Nobody Move, Nobody Get Hurt” and “It's a Hit”.

Brain Thrust Mastery (2008)

A more experimental album with electronic influences while keeping their signature indie rock style.

Barbara (2010)

A melodic and upbeat album featuring catchy guitar-driven tracks.

Helter Seltzer (2016)

An energetic album that continues the band's indie rock sound.

Band Members Profiles

Keith Murray

Keith is the lead vocalist and guitarist of We Are Scientists. He is known for his energetic performances and witty stage presence.

Chris Cain

Chris plays bass guitar and provides backing vocals. His basslines are the backbone of the band's signature indie rock sound.

Various Drummers

The band has worked with several talented touring drummers who contribute to their dynamic live performances.
